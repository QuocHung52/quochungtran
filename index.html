About Me

Who Am I?

Hello World,

My name is Quoc Hung Tran. I am a Software Engineer with a huge passion for building things and solving problems.

For the past few years, I have studied and worked in the embedded system. However, the more I dived into the embedded world, the more I realize this is not what I love. Ever since I moved to America, I have decided to study and try to become a great software engineer.

Before moving to America, I graduated from Ho Chi Minh University of Technology (one of the best colleges in the country) with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering.

In my last year of college, I joined the embedded engineering team at Global CyberSoft, Inc to develop and test R-Car starter kits for supporting automotive software development.
